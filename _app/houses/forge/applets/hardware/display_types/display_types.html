üîÑ Refresh Rate & Response Time

Refresh Rate (Hz)

How many times the display updates per second.

  • 60 Hz: Standard, office work
  • 75 Hz: Slight improvement
  • 144 Hz: Gaming standard
  • 240 Hz+: Competitive esports

Response Time (ms)

Time for a pixel to change colors.

  • 5ms: General use
  • 1-2ms: Gaming (reduces motion blur)
  • GTG: Gray-to-Gray measurement
  • MPRT: Moving Picture Response Time
‚ö° VRR Technologies: G-Sync (NVIDIA), FreeSync (AMD), and HDMI VRR sync the display refresh rate to GPU output, eliminating screen tearing.
