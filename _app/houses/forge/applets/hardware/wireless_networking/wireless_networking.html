üìç Wireless Network Types

Infrastructure Mode

  • Uses Access Point (AP)
  • Centralized management
  • Most common setup
  • AP bridges wireless to wired

Ad-Hoc Mode

  • Peer-to-peer, no AP
  • Direct device connection
  • Limited range/functionality
  • Also called IBSS
