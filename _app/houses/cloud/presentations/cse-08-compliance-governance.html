GDPR

General Data Protection Regulation

EU law protecting data privacy for individuals. Requires consent, data subject rights, privacy by design, and Data Protection Officers.

FISMA

Federal Information Security Management Act

U.S. federal law establishing cybersecurity standards for government tech infrastructure. Administered by DHS and OMB.

üéì

FERPA

Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act

Protects privacy of student education records. Gives parents/students rights to inspect, review, and request amendments.

Industry Compliance Standards

Sector-specific security requirements

Major Industry Standards

Payment

PCI-DSS

Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard for handling credit card data. 12 requirements across 6 goals.

Healthcare

HIPAA / HITRUST

Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act. Protects PHI with Privacy, Security, and Breach Notification Rules.

Financial

SOX / GLBA

Sarbanes-Oxley protects investors. GLBA regulates privacy/security of consumer financial information.
