üíé Fiber Optic Simulator

Visualize how light travels through single-mode and multi-mode fiber.

Single-Mode Fiber (SMF)

Core: 8-10¬µm | Jacket: Yellow

Characteristics:
  • Single light path - no bouncing
  • Minimal signal dispersion
  • Distance: 10km to 100km+
  • Requires laser light source
  • Higher cost, precision alignment

Multi-Mode Fiber (MMF)

Core: 50/62.5¬µm | Jacket: Orange/Aqua

Characteristics:
  • Multiple light paths - bounces
  • Modal dispersion limits distance
  • Distance: 300m to 2km
  • LED or VCSEL light source
  • Lower cost, easier to work with
