The Internet Protocol (IP) is responsible for addressing and routing packets of data so they can travel across networks and arrive at the correct destination. IP operates on a best-effort delivery model, meaning it does not guarantee delivery, order, or error checking of packets. Monitor IP for Address Spoofing, DDoS Attacks, and Routing Issues.
