Information Security Risk Management

Risk Treatment Options

Treatment Description When to Use
Mitigate Implement controls to reduce risk likelihood or impact Risk exceeds tolerance but can be reduced cost-effectively
Transfer Shift risk to third party (insurance, outsourcing) Risk cannot be mitigated internally, financial impact transferable
Avoid Eliminate the activity that creates the risk Risk too high, activity not essential to business
Accept Acknowledge risk and proceed without action Risk within tolerance, cost of treatment exceeds benefit
