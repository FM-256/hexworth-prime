Regulatory Compliance

Laws with penalties including fines and imprisonment

🇪🇺
GDPR
General Data Protection Regulation - EU data protection and privacy law
Scope: EU/EEA citizens' data
🇺🇸
FISMA
Federal Information Security Management Act - US government cybersecurity standards
Scope: US Federal agencies
🎓
FERPA
Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act - Student education records
Scope: Educational institutions

Industry Compliance Standards

Sector-specific security requirements

PCI-DSS
Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard for credit card data
HIPAA/HITRUST
Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act for protected health information
SOX
Sarbanes-Oxley Act protecting investors from fraudulent accounting

⚖ Cloud Security Governance

Ensuring cloud services are used securely and in compliance by: assigning clear roles and responsibilities, choosing appropriate frameworks (ISO 27001, NIST CSF, CSA CCM), implementing security controls based on risk assessment, and performing continuous assessment with audits and reviews.

💡 Key Takeaways

✓ Non-compliance = financial penalties + possible imprisonment
✓ GDPR, FISMA, FERPA are regulatory laws
✓ PCI-DSS, HIPAA, SOX are industry standards
✓ NIST CSF: Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover
✓ CSA CCM provides 197 control objectives in 17 domains
✓ Continuous monitoring is essential for compliance
